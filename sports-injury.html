Comprehensive Sports Injury Treatment in Delhi

Sports injuries can be devastating for athletes and active individuals, affecting performance, mobility, and quality of life. Dr. Bipin Solanki specializes in comprehensive sports injury treatment, combining his AIIMS trauma expertise with advanced orthopedic techniques to help patients return to their active lifestyles safely and effectively.

Understanding Sports Injuries

Sports injuries can occur due to acute trauma, overuse, or improper training techniques. They can affect muscles, tendons, ligaments, bones, and joints. Early diagnosis and appropriate treatment are crucial for optimal recovery and preventing long-term complications that could affect future athletic performance.

Common Sports Injuries We Treat

Dr. Bipin Solanki has extensive experience in treating various sports injuries:

Knee Injuries

  • ACL/PCL Tears: Anterior and posterior cruciate ligament injuries
  • Meniscus Tears: Cartilage damage in knee joint
  • Patellar Tendonitis: Jumper's knee
  • MCL/LCL Sprains: Medial and lateral collateral ligament injuries
  • Patellar Dislocation: Kneecap displacement
  • Iliotibial Band Syndrome: Runner's knee

Shoulder Injuries

  • Rotator Cuff Tears: Shoulder muscle and tendon damage
  • Shoulder Dislocation: Joint instability
  • Labral Tears: Cartilage damage in shoulder socket
  • Impingement Syndrome: Shoulder pain with overhead activities
  • Biceps Tendonitis: Inflammation of biceps tendon
  • Frozen Shoulder: Stiffness and limited range of motion

Ankle and Foot Injuries

  • Ankle Sprains: Ligament injuries
  • Achilles Tendonitis: Heel and calf pain
  • Plantar Fasciitis: Heel pain
  • Stress Fractures: Overuse bone injuries
  • Shin Splints: Lower leg pain
  • Turf Toe: Big toe joint injury

Hip and Groin Injuries

  • Hip Labral Tears: Cartilage damage in hip socket
  • Hip Flexor Strains: Muscle injuries
  • Sports Hernia: Groin pain and weakness
  • Hip Impingement: Bone abnormalities causing pain
  • IT Band Syndrome: Lateral hip and knee pain

Elbow and Wrist Injuries

  • Tennis Elbow: Lateral epicondylitis
  • Golfer's Elbow: Medial epicondylitis
  • Carpal Tunnel Syndrome: Wrist and hand numbness
  • Wrist Sprains: Ligament injuries
  • Ulnar Collateral Ligament Tears: Throwing injuries
