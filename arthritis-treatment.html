Comprehensive Arthritis Treatment in Delhi

Arthritis is a common condition that causes inflammation and pain in the joints, affecting millions of people worldwide. Dr. Bipin Solanki specializes in comprehensive arthritis treatment, offering both conservative and surgical solutions to help patients manage pain, improve joint function, and maintain an active lifestyle.

Understanding Arthritis

Arthritis is not a single disease but a term that covers over 100 different conditions affecting the joints and surrounding tissues. The most common symptoms include joint pain, stiffness, swelling, and reduced range of motion. Early diagnosis and treatment are crucial for preventing joint damage and maintaining quality of life.

Types of Arthritis We Treat

Dr. Bipin Solanki has extensive experience in treating various types of arthritis:

Osteoarthritis (OA)

  • Most common type of arthritis
  • Caused by wear and tear of joint cartilage
  • Affects weight-bearing joints (knees, hips, spine)
  • Progressive condition that worsens over time
  • Common in older adults and athletes

Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA)

  • Autoimmune condition affecting multiple joints
  • Causes inflammation of joint lining
  • Can affect other organs and systems
  • Requires specialized medical management
  • Early treatment prevents joint deformity

Psoriatic Arthritis

  • Associated with psoriasis skin condition
  • Affects joints and skin
  • Can cause nail changes and eye problems
  • Requires coordinated dermatological and orthopedic care

Gout

  • Caused by uric acid crystal buildup
  • Sudden, severe joint pain attacks
  • Commonly affects big toe, knees, ankles
  • Lifestyle and dietary modifications help
  • Medication can prevent future attacks

Ankylosing Spondylitis

  • Affects spine and large joints
  • Can cause spine fusion over time
  • Early diagnosis prevents disability
  • Exercise and medication management

Juvenile Arthritis

  • Arthritis in children and teenagers
  • Requires specialized pediatric care
  • Early treatment prevents growth problems
  • Family support and education crucial

Conservative Treatment Options

Dr. Bipin Solanki believes in trying conservative treatments first before considering surgery. Our comprehensive approach includes:

Medication Management

  • Pain relievers and anti-inflammatory drugs
  • Disease-modifying antirheumatic drugs (DMARDs)
  • Biologic response modifiers
  • Corticosteroids for inflammation control
  • Topical medications for localized pain

Physical Therapy & Exercise

  • Customized exercise programs
  • Range of motion exercises
  • Strengthening exercises
  • Low-impact aerobic activities
  • Aquatic therapy for joint protection

Lifestyle Modifications

  • Weight management and nutrition counseling
  • Activity modification and joint protection
  • Ergonomic workplace adjustments
  • Stress management techniques
  • Smoking cessation support

Joint Injections

  • Corticosteroid injections for inflammation
  • Hyaluronic acid injections for lubrication
  • Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy
  • Stem cell therapy for cartilage repair

Assistive Devices

  • Braces and splints for joint support
  • Walking aids and mobility devices
  • Orthotic shoe inserts
  • Adaptive equipment for daily activities

Alternative Therapies

  • Acupuncture for pain relief
  • Massage therapy for muscle relaxation
  • Heat and cold therapy
  • Yoga and tai chi for flexibility
  • Mindfulness and meditation

Prevention and Management

While some types of arthritis cannot be prevented, there are steps you can take to reduce your risk and manage symptoms:

  • Maintain Healthy Weight: Reduces stress on weight-bearing joints
  • Regular Exercise: Strengthens muscles and improves joint flexibility
  • Protect Your Joints: Use proper techniques during activities
  • Balanced Diet: Anti-inflammatory foods and proper nutrition
  • Regular Check-ups: Early detection and treatment
  • Stress Management: Reduces inflammation and pain
