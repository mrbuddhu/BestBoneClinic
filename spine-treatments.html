Spine Treatments Advanced

Comprehensive Spine Care and Treatment

Spine treatments encompass a wide range of procedures to address various spine conditions and injuries. Dr. Bipin Solanki, a leading orthopedic surgeon in Delhi, specializes in advanced spine treatments to help patients restore mobility and live pain-free.

What are Spine Treatments?

Spine treatments include both surgical and non-surgical approaches to address spine-related conditions. These treatments aim to relieve pain, restore function, and improve quality of life for patients suffering from spine disorders, injuries, or degenerative conditions.

Consult Now

Conditions Treated with Spine Surgery

  • Herniated Discs: Bulging or ruptured spinal discs
  • Spinal Stenosis: Narrowing of the spinal canal
  • Scoliosis: Abnormal spine curvature
  • Spine Fractures: Traumatic spine injuries
  • Degenerative Disc Disease: Age-related spine changes

Recovery & Rehabilitation

Recovery from spine treatments varies depending on the procedure performed. Physical therapy plays a crucial role in restoring strength and mobility. Most patients can return to daily activities within weeks to months, with full recovery depending on the specific treatment and individual factors.

What to Expect During the Procedure

Spine treatments may involve minimally invasive techniques or traditional surgery, depending on the condition. Dr. Bipin Solanki uses advanced imaging and surgical techniques to ensure precise treatment with minimal tissue damage and faster recovery times.
