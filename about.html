About Dr. Bipin Solanki

Senior Trauma & Orthopaedic Specialist with over two decades of excellence in patient care

Dr. Bipin Solanki

Senior Trauma & Orthopaedic Specialist

Dr. Bipin Solanki is a highly skilled and experienced trauma and orthopedic surgeon, specializing in fracture management, joint replacements, sports injuries, and advanced trauma care. With a patient-first approach, he utilizes cutting-edge trauma management techniques, minimally invasive procedures, and personalized rehabilitation plans to ensure the best possible recovery.

His expertise in complex trauma, joint replacement, arthroscopy, arthritis management, and sports injury treatment has helped thousands of patients regain mobility and lead pain-free lives.

Prestigious Background & Training

Dr. Solanki has served at the prestigious AIIMS Trauma Centre, Delhi, and holds advanced international training including the Advanced Trauma Management Course (England) and Primary & Revision Joint Replacement Training from Seoul, South Korea.
