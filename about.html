About Dr. Bipin Solanki
Best Trauma & Orthopaedic Specialist in Delhi NCR

Dr. Bipin Solanki is a highly skilled and experienced trauma and orthopedic surgeon, specializing in fracture management, joint replacements, sports injuries, and advanced trauma care. With a patient-first approach, he utilizes cutting-edge trauma management techniques, minimally invasive procedures, and personalized rehabilitation plans to ensure the best possible recovery.

Prestigious Background & Training

Dr. Solanki has served at the prestigious AIIMS Trauma Centre, Delhi, and holds advanced international training including the Advanced Trauma Management Course (England) and Primary & Revision Joint Replacement Training from Seoul, South Korea.

Comprehensive Expertise

His expertise in complex trauma, joint replacement, arthroscopy, arthritis management, and sports injury treatment has helped thousands of patients regain mobility and lead pain-free lives. From accident victims and elderly fracture cases to young athletes with sports injuries, Dr. Solanki combines global training, AIIMS trauma expertise, and compassionate care.

Patient-First Philosophy

At BBC Best Bone Clinic, we prioritize healing without unnecessary surgery, compassionate care, and evidence-based treatments. Dr. Solanki strongly believes in offering conservative treatments whenever possible, while being fully equipped with advanced skills for complex trauma and joint replacement cases.
