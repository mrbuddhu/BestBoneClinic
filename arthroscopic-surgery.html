Minimally Invasive Joint Procedures for Faster Recovery

Advanced Arthroscopic Surgery in Delhi

Arthroscopic surgery is a revolutionary minimally invasive technique that allows Dr. Bipin Solanki to diagnose and treat joint problems through tiny incisions using a specialized camera called an arthroscope. This advanced procedure offers numerous advantages over traditional open surgery, making it the preferred choice for many joint conditions.

What is Arthroscopic Surgery?

Arthroscopic surgery involves inserting a small camera (arthroscope) through a tiny incision to visualize the inside of a joint. This allows Dr. Solanki to examine the joint structures and perform surgical procedures with minimal tissue damage. The procedure is performed under anesthesia and typically takes 30-60 minutes depending on the complexity.

Benefits of Arthroscopic Surgery

  • Minimal Scarring: Small incisions (usually 5-10mm) result in barely visible scars
  • Faster Recovery: Reduced tissue damage leads to quicker healing and return to activities
  • Less Pain: Smaller incisions mean significantly less post-operative pain
  • Lower Risk: Reduced risk of infection, blood loss, and complications
  • Same Day Discharge: Most procedures allow same-day discharge
  • Precise Treatment: Enhanced visualization enables accurate diagnosis and targeted treatment
  • Shorter Hospital Stay: Reduced recovery time in hospital
  • Better Outcomes: Higher success rates with fewer complications

Recovery and Rehabilitation

Recovery from arthroscopic surgery is typically faster than traditional surgery. Most patients experience:

  • Immediate: Reduced pain and improved mobility within days
  • 1-2 Weeks: Return to daily activities and light work
  • 4-6 Weeks: Resume light exercise and physical therapy
  • 3-6 Months: Return to sports and strenuous activities (depending on procedure)
  • Long-term: Full recovery with minimal scarring
