Your Privacy Matters

Protecting Your Personal Information

BBC Best Bone Clinic is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in accordance with applicable laws and medical privacy standards.

We understand the sensitive nature of medical information and are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of privacy protection. Your trust is paramount to us, and we ensure that all personal and medical data is handled with the utmost care and security.

Our Privacy Commitments

  • Confidentiality of all medical information
  • Secure Storage of personal data
  • Limited Access to authorized personnel only
  • Patient Consent for information sharing
  • Legal Compliance with privacy laws
  • Regular Updates to privacy practices

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, date of birth, emergency contacts
  • Medical Information: Medical history, diagnosis, treatment plans, medications
  • Insurance Information: Insurance provider, policy numbers, coverage details
  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited (for website analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing medical care and treatment
  • Communicating with you about appointments and care
  • Processing insurance claims and billing
  • Improving our services and patient care
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Emergency situations and public health requirements

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Medical Necessity: With other healthcare providers involved in your care
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • Public Health: For disease reporting and public health purposes
  • Emergency Situations: When necessary to protect your health or safety

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Staff training on privacy and security
  • Physical security measures for paper records
  • Backup and disaster recovery procedures

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your medical records
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Restriction: Request limitations on how we use your information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your information to another provider
  • Complaint: File a complaint about our privacy practices

6. Retention of Information

We retain your medical records and personal information in accordance with:

  • Legal requirements for medical record retention
  • Professional standards for healthcare providers
  • Our internal policies and procedures
  • Your ongoing care needs

7. Website Privacy

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Information you provide through contact forms
  • Appointment booking information
  • Website usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Cookies for website functionality

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.
